The Doggo Squad.

Meet The Doggo Squad (TDS), the most elite army squads in the world. TDS only answers to one guy, their commander, Major Gud Boi. They have carried out some of the most covert operations in the world, including:

  • The capture and detainment of world-known terrorist, Cat Nyau III. Sought for years by the government.
  • Decimation of the Squirrel Militia in the Republic of Msitu.
  • Stoppage of meat trafficking along the coast of Wacanda.
  • Various peace-keeping missions in the human world, only armed with special training from the less-intelligent human race.
